Terms and Conditions

These terms summarize how reservations, vehicle classes, trip changes, and final confirmations are handled.

Please note

  • Final trip terms belong inside the confirmed quote or contract
  • Vehicle images represent class, not always the exact unit
  • Extra time and route changes can affect billing

Reservations

Reservations should only be treated as confirmed after written acceptance of the quote and completion of any deposit, agreement, or payment requirements listed for the service.

Vehicle classes

Requested vehicles are quoted by service class. Exact models, layouts, and amenities can vary based on the assigned unit and the operational needs of the trip.

Trip changes

Additional stops, waiting time, schedule adjustments, route changes, airport delays, and onsite coordination can affect final billing depending on the trip type and service level.

Cancellations

Cancellation windows and any applicable fees depend on the confirmed service, vehicle class, timing, and supplier commitments. The exact policy will be listed on the approved quote or contract for your reservation.

Passenger conduct

Passengers are expected to follow safety rules, vehicle conduct guidelines, and any restrictions on smoking, damage, or prohibited items communicated during booking.

Priority of documents

If there is ever a difference between the public website summary and a signed contract, the signed contract or accepted written quote should control the final reservation terms.
