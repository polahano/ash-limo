Privacy Policy

This policy summarizes what information the website collects, why it is collected, and how it supports quoting, booking, support, and analytics.

Collected information

  • Name, phone, email, and trip details from forms
  • Optional notes about flights, venues, and itineraries
  • Basic website analytics and device information
  • Communication history when you contact the business

How information is used

Lead data is used to respond to quote requests, confirm reservations, coordinate service, send updates, and improve the website experience.

Operational systems

Information may be shared with trusted software providers or service partners when needed to support reservations, dispatch, payment processing, analytics, or customer communication.

Data sharing

Information should only be shared with service providers, software vendors, or partners when needed to support reservations, dispatch, payments, compliance, or analytics.

Retention

Trip and contact data should be retained only as long as needed for service delivery, records, compliance, customer support, or legitimate business operations.

Your choices

You can request updates to your contact information, ask questions about stored trip data, or ask the business to explain how your inquiry is being handled.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the business directly at info@ashlimoandcharterbuses.com or call (682) 551-2910.
